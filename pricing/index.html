How do you estimate project costs?

Each project starts with a discovery phase where we:

  • Analyze your brief and goals to understand scope, priorities, and business objectives.
  • Define deliverables and timelines where we outline every stage of work and expected outcomes.
  • Select the right team composition.

You’ll receive a detailed estimate:

  • Tasks and deliverables per phase
  • Estimated hours and timelines
  • Team structure
  • Cost
  • Optional add-ons (branding, motion, QA, support, etc.)

This clarity helps you plan budgets confidently, avoid scope creep, and reduce over-budgeting risks by up to 30%.

How quickly can you start after approval?

We can usually start within 3–5 business days (and even faster in some cases). Once we sign an agreement, the EliTechWiz team immediately:

  • Assign a project manager and core team.
  • Set up your workspace in Slack, Notion, or Trello for transparent communication and provide access to your project files in Figma to see everything in real time.
  • Have an alignment session to finalize scope, priorities, and next steps.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Of course, EliTechWiz provides comprehensive post-launch support if you request it, to help your product grow (because we care about your success).

We can continue working with you on:

  • Technical maintenance

This support helps our clients keep product performance, reduce churn by 35%, and sustain growth long after launch.
