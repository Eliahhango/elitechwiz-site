What is the EliTechWiz Referral Program, and who can join?

The EliTechWiz Referral Program is a partnership model for businesses that want to monetize unused leads or expand their service range without increasing operational load. You can refer clients who need high-quality strategic design services and earn 5 –10% commission from each successful project.The program also works both ways. We share leads outside our core expertise with our trusted partners.Our referral program is ideal for:

  • Design agencies looking to scale capacity
  • Development teams missing in-house product design
  • Consulting and marketing companies seeking a reliable design partner
  • Freelancers or solopreneurs wanting to generate passive income from their networks

What types of design projects can I refer to EliTechWiz?

You can refer any digital product or brand design project that requires a professional, full-cycle approach. EliTechWiz delivers a wide range of services trusted by 250+ global businesses across various industries:

  • Pitch Deck
  • Branding/rebranding
  • Logo design
  • Graphic design
  • UX audit
  • UI/UX design
  • Web design
  • Mobile app design
  • Redesign
  • Web development
  • MVP development
  • WebFlow development
  • Landing page design and development
  • Mobile development

All referrals benefit from our award-winning design standards, transparent delivery process, and results measured in growth, conversion, and faster go-to-market.

Can we work under my brand, or create co-branded cases together?

You can choose the collaboration format that best suits your business model.

  • If you prefer full confidentiality, we work under your brand as a White Label partner. We sign an NDA and act as part of your in-house team. We handle design delivery behind the scenes.
  • If you’re open to joint visibility, we can collaborate openly as a partner and create co-branded case studies or portfolio materials that highlight the shared success. We develop every co-case by agreement and align with your brand positioning. It helps both sides strengthen reputation and attract new clients (and is a good option for sharing leads).

When and how do I receive my referral payments?

You’ll receive referral payments after the client completes payment for the project. EliTechWiz ensures full transparency and fairness.
‍Your commission: 10% for each project you refer to us.

‍There are no hidden fees or delays. Once we receive the client’s payment, your commission is processed promptly with a clear report.

Do I need to manage or stay involved in the referred projects?

No. Once you refer a project, our EliTechWiz team takes full responsibility for communication, delivery, and client management. Our project team handles everything: discovery, research, design, and handoff. You don’t need to coordinate tasks, join meetings, or oversee progress unless you choose to stay informed.Your role ends at the referral stage. We manage operations, deadlines, and quality standards when you earn commissions without extra workload or hidden obligations.

How fast can we launch the partnership or pilot project?

We can start collaboration within a few days after our initial call. The process is simple and transparent:

  • Intro call. We discuss expertise, ideal client types, and preferred collaboration model (White Label or Co-Branded).
  • Alignment. We synchronize processes, tone of voice, SLAs, and lead attribution.
  • Pilot stage. We begin with a small project or a referred lead to test the workflow and communication.

Most partners fully launch the program and first referral within 1-2 weeks, with all terms, NDAs, and payment details finalized upfront.
