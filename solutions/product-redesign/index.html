Frequently
asked questions

What is a Product Redesign service?

Redesign is a strategic approach to improving an existing digital product. Whether you're looking for an iOS redesign agency, Android redesign agency, or web redesign agency, the goal is to improve appearance, user experience, functionality, and overall performance. We aim to make the product more competitive, user-friendly, and aligned with specific business goals. The processes typically involve considering user feedback, optimizing conversion paths, and providing mobile responsiveness and accessibility. Testing and continuous iteration are critical components of this service.

When should you consider a redesign?

Consider changes when your product looks outdated and integrate them into your redesign process. Also, pay attention to low user retention, past redesigns failed, poor conversion rates, lag behind competitors, and lack of user engagement. A redesign is a strategic move to refresh and align your product with current user and market expectations.

How much does it cost to redesign a product?

The cost of a product redesign depends on numerous factors. These include the scope of changes, complexity, team expertise, research, timeline, new features, platform compatibility, quality assurance, and post-launch support. Costs vary significantly based on these aspects. So get in touch with our web/app redesign agency experts, who will advise you on your project’s budget.

How to measure the success of a new product design?

Measuring the success of a redesign process involves measuring KPIs and user-centric metrics. Key consideration areas include the following:
- User satisfaction
- User engagement
- Conversion rates
- User retention
- Usability and performance
- Customer feedback
- Competitive positioning
- Alignment with business goals
- Iterative improvements
- User adoption
- User experience metrics
Successful redesign aligns with business objectives, encourages user adoption, and results in positive user experiences.
