FAQ

What industries do you specialize in?

EliTechWiz is a B2B website design agency that specializes in AI, Fintech, Web3, HealthTech, and other industries. Selling software, offering compliance solutions to financial teams, or pitching complex services to enterprise buyers? We know how to position your product for decision-makers.

Our experts design with your real sales process in focus and pay attention to long cycles, complex user paths, and multiple decision-makers so your website actually supports how B2B deals get done.

How long does a project usually take?

Most web design and development projects take from 3 to 12+ weeks. But before saying the exact timeline, our team analyzes how complex the site is, how many pages you need, and who’s involved on your side.

That’s why we start with a discovery phase to map out the scope, timeline, and key milestones and present it to you so you’ll know exactly what to expect, with no surprises.

Will our team be able to edit the site?

Absolutely! Our B2B website development company prioritizes long-term usability. We build websites with user-friendly CMS platforms (Webflow or WordPress) so your dev, marketing, or content teams can easily update copy, images, blog posts, or landing pages without our help (but if you need - no problem, our devs will do it fast).

We also provide hands-on CMS training, easy-to-edit templates, and ongoing support because we care about your team feeling confident and entirely in control of the site after launch.

What platform do you recommend for B2B websites?

We recommend platforms based on your business goals, team size, and future scaling plans. For many B2B companies, Webflow is a good option. It offers flexibility, fast performance, and easy content editing. WordPress is also a solid choice if you need robust plugins and SEO customization.For larger enterprises or custom workflows, our dev team suggests Headless CMS solutions for maximum scalability and performance.
No worries, in our B2B website development agency, we have a discovery phase. During this initial stage, we evaluate your tech stack, team capabilities, and growth roadmap to help you choose the most strategic platform.

Can I update my website content on my own after launch?

Yes. One of our main goals is to make sure your team stays in control after launch. That’s why we build on user-friendly CMS platforms like Webflow or WordPress. Don’t worry, your marketing or content team can easily update copy, images, blog posts, and service pages without relying on a developer.

Our B2B web development agency also offer onboarding and training to help your team hit the ground running from day one. You get flexibility, speed, and full ownership over your digital presence. And we always stay in touch!

Do you handle A/B testing for landing pages?

Yes, A/B testing is a part of our optimization strategy. We help you test different versions of your landing pages, headlines, CTAs, layout variations, and user flows to understand what drives more clicks, conversions, or signups. Our team analyzes real user behavior, uses various tools to identify what works best, and provides clear insights to guide future decisions and improve ROI.

Can you design both the website and the pitch deck together?

Of course. Your website and pitch deck need to tell a consistent, compelling story, especially when targeting investors or enterprise buyers. That’s why we offer business-to-business design services that cover both assets in parallel.

Our design teams align messaging, visuals, and user experience to create strong online presence and presentation materials. They must reinforce each other. You’ll get a website that converts and a pitch deck that close deals (all from our expert team).

Can I start with a smaller package and scale later?

Yes! Many of our clients begin with a focused engagement (a UX audit or landing page design) to test impact and fit. Then, expand to other services like product redesign, mobile design, or development. Your needs evolve - our services scale with you.

You can expand into full-site design, branding, development, or CRO at any time. Our flexible approach ensures you only invest in what makes sense for your current growth stage.

How do you ensure fast communication with teams?

We prioritize clear, fast, and flexible communication. From the first day of cooperation, you’ll get a dedicated project manager who guides the process and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

You’ll also have direct access to our design team. There are no middle layers and no delays. We set up shared Slack channels for quick questions and feedback, schedule regular check-ins to align on progress, and offer on-demand calls for deeper discussions if needed.

We share all working files transparently via Figma (for real-time updates and comments) and Notion. You’ll also receive access to your evolving design system. It includes the style guide and UI components, so your team always knows what’s happening and where things stand.

Will I have direct access to my designer or developer?

Yes. As a client of our B2B web agency, you’ll have direct access to your designer, developer, and a dedicated project manager every step of the way.

We set up shared Slack channels for fast communication, give you access to Figma for real-time design collaboration, and hold regular check-ins to keep everyone aligned. Your project manager ensures timelines, deliverables, and priorities stay on track. And your direct line to the design team keeps feedback loops quick and clear.
