FAQ

How much time is needed for corporate website design?

To define an exact timeline, our corporate website design agency takes into account the scale of your business, the number of stakeholders involved, and the complexity of your digital ecosystem. Based on our experience, a corporate website design project typically takes between 6 and 14 weeks.

  • 4-6 weeks → Average timeline for mid-sized corporate websites with 10–15 core pages (About, Services, Case Studies, Blog, Careers). Our processes include a discovery phase with market, competitor, and user research, wireframing, UI/UX design, responsiveness, and visual identity alignment. Deliverables cover wireframes, moodboards, full-page designs, and a UI kit.
  • 10-12 weeks → Average timeline for larger corporations that have multiple business units represented, or complex content hierarchies. You’ll get wireframes, moodboards, full-page designs, UI kit, scalable design systems according to the accessibility standards (WCAG/ADA), and responsive layouts across all devices.
  • 12-14+ weeks → It’s our average timeline if you need strict compliance, industry-specific regulations if your project is in finance, healthcare, or legal niche, or custom integrations (CRM, ERP, or intranet portals). Additional deliverables you’ll get include stakeholder workshops, design documentation for developers, and a complete enterprise-ready design system.

What is the difference between small business websites and corporate website design? If it’s a corporate project, then our design team communicates with your marketing, IT, compliance, and leadership teams to consider the nuances and schedule feedback rounds and approvals. So, we must create a clear roadmap to deliver a high-quality product that scales with your business without delays.If you want to know the exact time your project needs, contact our team.

What's the price for building my corporate website?

The cost of a corporate website depends on the scope. To answer this question, we need to know:

  • How large is your website?
  • Do you need only corporate website design service, only development, or a full design + development package?
  • What is your industry? (to know if there are strict compliance requirements or not).

We customize pricing to your company’s size, compliance needs, and integrations. But let’s provide you with average ranges:

  • Design only. From $10K for mid-sized corporate sites (10–20 pages). It includes UI/UX design, brand consistency, responsive layouts, and design system creation. For complex multi-branch businesses, the price is higher.
  • Development only. From $20K, and it depends on the technology stack, CMS, and custom features (ERP, CRM, or intranet integrations). Enterprise-level development requires strict security and advanced architecture. So, the price can reach $50K+.
  • Full design + development. The price starts from $30K and depends on complexity, industry, and scope. This full-cycle service includes research, UI/UX, scalable architecture, accessibility, QA, CMS integration, and cross-department alignment.
  • Continuous support and optimization. Many corporations allocate $2K-$5K monthly for ongoing updates, performance monitoring, and compliance checks.

To know the exact price for your project, contact our team. We’ll calculate every process in detail, and the estimate is completely free.

Also, take into account that your corporate platform needs legal reviews, accessibility compliance, and integration with enterprise tools. It helps your website look professional, scale globally, and meet standards.

What services are included in corporate website development?

When you work with our professional corporate website developers, you’ll get:

  • Frontend engineering. You’ll get a clean, maintainable code. Your website will look and perform exactly as designed across browsers and devices.
  • Backend development. You’ll get a robust architecture, database management, and API integrations with your ERP, CRM, or other enterprise systems. Your website will become a high-quality business tool.
  • CMS implementation. You’ll get a setup and customization of enterprise-ready systems like WordPress, Sitecore, Webflow, or Adobe Experience Manager. We will help you choose the best solution for your business needs that will make your content easy to manage across teams, markets, and regions.
  • Security and compliance. You’ll get an enterprise-level encryption, access control, and adherence to global standards (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2). We take care that your data and reputation stay protected.
  • Optimization and QA. You’ll get performance tuning, load testing, and accessibility compliance (WCAG/ADA). So, the platform will remain fast, inclusive, and reliable under enterprise traffic.
  • Post-launch support (optional). We can help you with continuous monitoring, maintenance, and optimization to keep your website secure and effective.

One important note! Website development is the foundation of a reliable and scalable digital platform. And it can only succeed with a strong design in place. If you don’t yet have enterprise-ready UI/UX, we can help you with this first.

How much input will our staff have during website creation?

Building a corporate website is a collaborative process. But no worries, your staff won’t be burdened with technical or large amounts of tasks because we value your time and understand you are busy. Our team needs their input across key stages only to make sure the final product reflects your business goals, compliance needs, and brand authority. Here’s what it can look like:

Stage 1. Discovery
‍You share insights on business goals, compliance standards, market positioning, requirements, and nuances we need to know about your project. Our team then conducts competitor analysis and target audience research. So, your staff provides strategic alignment, and the EliTechWiz team translates it into actionable website requirements.

Stage 2. Wireframes and prototyping
‍Your staff reviews wireframes and clickable prototypes. User testing can also involve internal stakeholders to validate clarity and usability. The benefit for you is that you see the structure early, catch gaps, and confirm the website meets user expectations before our developers write code.

Stage 3. Visual design
‍Marketing and brand teams participate in moodboard reviews, concept presentations, and layout approvals. Your staff input here guarantees the design is on-brand, responsive, and supported by a UI style guide. You will see brand consistency across all regions and devices.

Stage 4. Development and deployment
‍Your IT or compliance teams provide input on integrations, data security, and legal requirements. If you don’t have these teams, we will handle this as well. After development and QA, staff participate in user acceptance testing before deployment. You’ll get a website that meets corporate standards without development bottlenecks.

Stage 5. Support stage
‍Post-launch, your staff collaborates with us only for updates, performance analysis, and iterative improvements. CMS training sessions also empower your content team to manage updates independently. The benefit for you is that your staff gains autonomy but has expert support on call for complex needs.

In short, your staff will engage only where their input drives business value (strategy, brand, compliance). And our design and/or development team will handle execution and optimization.

What measures guarantee our business site is secure and search-optimized?

Security and visibility are the two pillars of our main approaches to corporate website creation. We combine enterprise-grade protection with SEO best practices to make your site safe and discoverable.

Enterprise security
‍We protect your website with:

  • SSL certificates
  • Data encryption
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Regular vulnerability scanning
  • Access controls (only approved staff can make changes)
  • GDPR, HIPAA, or SOC2 standards when required

With this approach, your brand will avoid costly breaches, fines, and reputation damage

Infrastructure and monitoring

  • Firewalls
  • DDoS protection
  • 24/7 uptime monitoring

This makes your site resilient under heavy traffic or malicious attempts. We also recommend conducting post-deployment audits and penetration testing to provide ongoing security assurance.

Technical and content SEO

  • Clean code
  • Fast load times
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • Structured data
  • Metadata management
  • URL optimization

Your every page must be search-friendly, and we’ll handle this as well.

Continuous optimization
‍After launch, we can conduct performance monitoring to identify issues early.

After all these stages, your business website will become a secure and high-performing asset that clients trust and search engines reward. It’ll help you drive measurable traffic and qualified leads.
