FAQ

How do you ensure brand consistency during a rebrand?

Our rebrand agency follows a structured and strategic process for consistent branding across every channel and interaction. It starts with a comprehensive brand audit to identify what’s working, what’s not, and what needs to evolve.

From there, we define a clear visual language (logo variations, color palette, typography, iconography, brand patterns, and messaging principles).We then document everything in detailed brand guidelines to ensure that anyone on your team can apply the brand correctly and confidently.

Will I be involved in the creative process?

Of course! Great rebranding happens through collaboration.  At EliTechWiz,  you’re involved from day one,  and we keep communication clear and transparent throughout the entire process.

Here’s how it looks:
  • Kickoff & discovery.  We start with discovery sessions to understand your business,  values,  audience,  and goals.  Your insights and vision build the foundation of the project.
  • Dedicated project manager.  You’ll have a direct point of contact who keeps everything organized and moving forward.  No chasing updates!
  • Daily reports & time tracking.  Regular updates and transparent time logs so you always know what’s happening and when.
  • Weekly meetings & feedback loops.  We schedule check-ins to share progress,  present concepts,  and gather your feedback to make the direction feel right at every stage.
  • Access to all working files.  You’ll have full visibility into designs,  Figma files,  and documentation in real time.
  • Final presentation & guidelines.  Before handoff,  we walk you through all final assets and brand guidelines for clarity and alignment moving forward.

We care that the process is collaborative, flexible, and easy to manage on your end.

What factors influence the price of a rebrand?

The cost of a rebrand depends on several key factors:

  • Project scope.  Are we refreshing just the logo and color palette or building a full brand system with marketing materials,  website redesign,  and brand strategy?
  • Level of research.  Extensive competitor analysis,  user research,  and market positioning require more time and strategic thinking.
  • Visual assets. The more brand elements you need (icons,  illustrations,  animations, branded print materials,  etc.),  the more design hours it takes.
  • Deliverables.  Do you need brand guidelines,  templates,  UI design,  or development-ready files?  The number and complexity of deliverables influence the final cost.
  • Urgency & timelines.  Tight deadlines may require a bigger team for faster turnaround.

We’re transparent about pricing from the start and always help you prioritize what brings the most value.  Want to know what your rebrand might cost? Contact us and we’ll provide a custom estimate based on your goals.

How do I know it’s the right time to rebrand?

There are a few clear signs that your brand may be holding you back. If you see one of these problems, it’s time to consider a rebrand:

  • Drop in user engagement.
  • Low conversion rates (especially below industry benchmarks).
  • Inconsistent visuals across platforms.
  • Outdated design that no longer reflects your product.
  • Lower KPIs.

Other indicators are:

  • Shift in business goals.
  • Entering new markets.
  • Lack of emotional connection with your target audience.

To help you make a confident decision,  we offer a free brand audit for qualifying projects.  In it,  we assess visual consistency,  messaging clarity,  brand perception, and user interaction.  We care about our client’s success,  so we want to give you a clear snapshot of where your brand stands and what needs to evolve.

How do you measure success?

We define success by how well your new brand performs across key areas: user engagement, conversion rates,  brand recognition,  and overall market positioning.

Here’s how it looks:
  • Increased time on site and reduced bounce rate after launch.
  • Higher brand recall and improved user perception.
  • Growth in conversion rates (sign-ups,  inquiries,  purchases).
  • Positive client and customer feedback.
  • Brand consistency across all touchpoints.

Beyond the numbers, success means your brand finally feels right!  This means it’s aligned with your vision,  goals,  and audience.  And we’re here to support you long after launch to make sure it continues bringing excellent results.

Will you help implement the new branding across my channels?

Yes,  we don’t stop at design delivery.  As a full-service design agency,  we help you implement your new branding across all key touchpoints.  This includes updating your website and app,  social media templates,  email campaigns,  and preparing branded assets for presentations,  ads,  and printed materials. 

Our development team can also help transform a new branding into the functional,  scalable, and live-ready product.  Or we can support your marketing,  design,  and development teams during the rollout phase.
