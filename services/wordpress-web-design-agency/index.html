FAQ

How long does it take to design and develop a WordPress website?

Your project can take from 4 to 12 weeks because it depends on the scope, complexity, and readiness of your content.

We’ll start with the discovery stage and form the strategy to gather and organize your business goals, user needs, brand guidelines, and technical requirements. It’s about 1 week.

What steps will be next?

  • UX/UI design. Our designers craft wireframes and visual layouts and it takes 2–3 weeks.
  • Development. After design approval, our devs build a responsive, SEO-optimized WordPress website (custom or based on a chosen theme). It takes 2–6 weeks.
  • Testing and QA. Your site goes through rigorous browser, device, and speed testing (about a week).
  • Launch and training. Our dev team will help you go live in the digital world and train your team to manage the CMS. In general, this process lasts 1 week.

If you need integrations (for example, CRM, marketing tools, APIs) or custom features (gated content or dashboards), this can extend the timeline. But we’ll provide a precise project plan upfront. So you don’t need to worry about delays. Our processes are transparent: you’ll get real-time updates, and our project manager will keep you informed.

Can you design my website to match my brand guidelines?

Absolutely. Our WordPress web design company will translate your brand identity into a consistent, on-brand digital experience.

It means that your:

  • Typography
  • Color palette
  • Logo usage
  • Tone of voice
  • Visual hierarchy

directly match your brand guidelines. Every page follows your brand system and enhances usability and conversions.

If you don’t have formal brand guidelines yet, our brand designers will create them or refine your existing assets for digital use.

Do you offer responsive/mobile-first web design?

Yes, 100%. EliTechWiz, WordPress design agency, prioritizes mobile-first and responsive design in every project.

Mobile-first design means our expert team starts to design for smaller screens. It’s important for:

  • Fast load times
  • Clear navigation
  • Easy interaction on mobile devices.

Responsive design means your site automatically adapts to any screen size. No matter what it is - phone, tablet, or desktop.
Why is it so important?

This approach:

  • Improves usability by up to 80%
  • Boosts SEO rankings by 15–30%
  • Helps convert users across all devices with up to 2× higher mobile conversion rates.

Can I request changes during the project?

Of course, you can request changes during the project, and we’ve structured our process to make that both smooth and collaborative.Depending on the service, our packages include several concepts at the start and 1–2 revision rounds during the process free of charge.

First of all, we’ll have the discovery phase to understand your brand, goals, and vision in detail. The better information, vision, and wishes you describe at the beginning, the fewer major changes we’ll need later. This helps avoid miscommunication and keeps the project on schedule.

You'll get full access to your live Figma file. There, you can see our designers’ work as it evolves, step by step, and follow the process in real-time.

Small tweaks are also okay along the way because we care about the final product to feel right to you.

Overall, we aim for close collaboration and full visibility. We consider your input as one of the most important parts of building a website that works for your business and delivers great results.

Can I update my website content on my own after launch?

Yes, and we’ll make sure you feel confident doing it.
Our WordPress web development company builds websites with intuitive, user-friendly CMS setups. That means your team can easily update content:

  • Text
  • Images
  • Blog posts
  • Service pages.

No developer needed.
After launch you’ll get a hands-on walkthrough and optional training session with our developer. He shows you exactly how to manage your site. You’ll learn how to edit pages, upload media, create posts, and handle basic site functions.
But if you need help down the line, we can always support you.

Can I use my own fonts, images, or videos?

Of course, you can, and we’ll help you make the most of them.
You can bring your own brand assets (fonts, images, and videos) and your site will reflect your unique identity. During the design phase, we’ll review these assets together. It helps us understand whether they meet performance, quality, and licensing standards. If anything needs improvement, our brand designers will guide you on how to optimize it.
Our goal is to integrate your visuals to look professional, on-brand, and impressive. But if you need help filling gaps, our design team can suggest or source high-quality visuals to keep everything cohesive.

Can you integrate third-party tools?

Absolutely. As part of our WordPress website development services, we integrate a wide range of third-party tools to support your business goals.

  • CRM platforms (e.g., HubSpot or Salesforce)
  • Email marketing tools (e.g., Mailchimp)
  • Analytics dashboards (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Payment gateways (e.g., Stripe or PayPal)
  • Automation tools (e.g., Zapier)

Our team will evaluate compatibility, ensure secure API connections, and test functionality across all key pages and user flows. You’ll get a fully connected digital ecosystem with smooth data flows and automated processes. Your team will save time and get actionable insights.

What tools do you use to manage projects and approvals?

We use different modern tools to keep your project organized, transparent, and efficient from start to finish.

  • Notion for timelines, roadmaps, and shared documentation
  • Slack for direct, fast communication and async updates
  • Figma for real-time design collaboration, feedback, and approvals
  • Loom for walkthroughs, design reviews, and async explanations
  • Google Meet or Zoom for regular check-ins and milestone reviews

No worries! You’ll always get full visibility into the process, clear feedback cycles, and no surprises. Also, our project manager will keep everything on track, keep in touch with you, and is always ready to answer any questions or provide updates.

Will I have direct access to my designer or developer?

Of course. We at EliTechWiz, a WordPress website development agency, prioritize clear and direct communication throughout your entire project.
Here’s what working with us feels like ↓

  • You’ll chat directly with your designer or developer on Slack or email. No waiting, no layers.
  • Your project manager keeps everything on track so nothing slips through the cracks.
  • We’ll hop on regular Zoom or Google Meet check-ins to walk you through progress and next steps.
  • You’ll have real-time access to live Figma files. Watch your site come to life as we design.
  • Everything we decide together lives in a shared Notion doc. So you never lose sight of updates, feedback, or deadlines.

We value and provide full transparency, clear communication, and treat your project like a true collaboration because we really care about your success. You’re always connected to the people building your site.

Can you optimize my WordPress site for speed and performance?

Absolutely. We focus on speed and performance in every WordPress project.

Our optimization process includes fast, lightweight coding, image compression, lazy loading, and clean theme architecture to keep your site lean. We also use caching, database optimization, and performance plugins to make your website load fast.

What will you get?

  • Sites that load in under 2 seconds
  • Improved SEO rankings
  • Lower bounce rates
  • Higher conversions (especially on mobile)

We value and provide full transparency, clear communication, and treat your project like a true collaboration because we really care about your success. You’re always connected to the people building your site.

Can you migrate my existing website to WordPress without losing SEO?

Yes, we can migrate your existing website to WordPress and fully preserve your SEO performance.

Our SEO-safe migration process includes:

  • URL structure mapping to retain existing links or set up proper redirects
  • Metadata transfer (titles, descriptions, alt texts) to protect your search rankings
  • Preservation of on-page SEO elements (like header tags and internal linking)
  • Content migration with no duplication or loss

Our team makes sure your traffic, rankings, and domain authority remain intact or even improved after migration.

Do you provide ongoing support or maintenance after the site goes live?

Of course! We offer ongoing support and maintenance to keep your WordPress site secure, up-to-date, and performing at its best after launch.

What you can expect:

  • Regular updates for WordPress core, themes, and plugins
  • Performance checks and speed optimization
  • Backups and uptime monitoring
  • Technical support for bug fixes, minor changes, and questions
  • Content assistance (upon request) to help your team manage the site

Our team is your long-term partner (not just your launch team), so you can rely on us during scaling, and we’ll take all your tech support on us.
