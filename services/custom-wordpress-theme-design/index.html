FAQ

Can WordPress themes be customized?

Yes! Customizing your WordPress theme means your website will look different and more professional in comparison with most templated websites. It will function exactly as your business needs it to. You will also have better speed, SEO security, and user experience to keep customers coming back to convert. 

EliTechWiz is an expert WordPress design agency that specializes in fully customized themes. They match perfectly with your brand, user journey, and business goals. Need to change the layout, create unique functionality, add custom integrations, or improve performance? Our designers can create a customized solution built to scale with you. Get control of your design, speed, and scalability today!

What tools do you use for project management and collaboration?

We keep tasks and processes organized, transparent, and easy to follow. For project management, EliTechWiz uses Trello or Jira (it just depends on what your team prefers). This will help you manage deadlines, tasks, and keep the coordinated part of your project on track.

For collaboration in real time, we use Slack, Zoom or Google Meet. You will also have access to a shared Figma file, so you can see how designs are progressing, leave comments, and we can review progress in real time. Our designers and developers connect with your assigned project manager to provide updates, get feedback rounds in, and to initiate quality sync calls so everyone is kept aligned. And no matter if we’re working with your marketing team, developers, or leadership.

Do you already have preferred tools? No problem! We do our best to accommodate our partners. The main goal is to keep you up to date without flooding your inbox.

What is the best WordPress theme builder?

It depends on your needs. But if performance, flexibility, and scalability matter to your business, our EliTechWiz team usually recommends avoiding generic theme builders altogether.

Elementor, Divi, or WPBakery tools can be helpful for quick setups or smaller websites. But pay attention that they often come with bloated code, slower load times, and limited design control.

If your company is a growth-focused company, then the best approach our experts recommend is a custom theme, created for your brand and business logic. It gives you full creative freedom, better SEO performance, faster speeds, and long-term flexibility without relying on third-party builders. Our WordPress design company will tailor each solution to your goals.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Of course. We offer ongoing support and maintenance to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and working perfectly. What do we mean by support? Plugin updates, performance monitoring, bug fixes, backups, and quick help when you need changes or improvements. You get a long-term partner who’s genuinely invested in your success because when your product grows, so does our impact.

How do you ensure your designs align with accessibility standards (ADA/WCAG)?

Accessibility is at the foundational level of the quality we offer. Your WordPress project will adhere to the latest ADA Standards and WCAG 2.1 (AA level) right from the start to make your site work for everyone. 

Important note! Accessible websites reach a wider audience, provide a better user experience, reduce liability, and are favored by search engines for delivery. Our WordPress designers and developers care not only about compliance, but also about your users, as customer-centered design is our main approach to creating better products. 

How we do it:

  • We provide users with high color contrast, adequate font size for readability, visual hierarchy of content, and navigation for mouse and keyboard users during the design phase. We do not rely solely on color as a means of communication. We clearly label interactive elements and make them easy to activate. 
  • During development, we will use semantic HTML and proper heading structure, such as ARIA labelled where necessary. All of your site's components (forms, buttons, menus, etc.) will be operable and applicable via screen readers and keyboard controls. Our team tests your site on a range of devices and with various assistive technologies to identify real-world issues before launch.

Can you help us define or refine our digital brand identity?

Absolutely! Our brand designers provide branding services. It can also be a part of our process. We’ll define or refine your digital brand identity to meet your business goals and resonate with your audience.

Are you building something new? EliTechWiz team can guide you from the very beginning and develop your brand strategy, values, positioning, messaging, and visual identity. 

Are you rebranding? We assess what to keep, what to improve, and how to make your identity more modern, consistent, and scalable.

You’ll receive a complete brand identity package:

  • Logo design and usage variations
  • Color palette
  • Typography system
  • Icon and illustration styles
  • UI elements for digital use
  • Brand guidelines for consistency across all touchpoints

How do you handle responsive theme design for mobile and tablet?

Given the reality that most traffic to your website comes from mobile devices, the standard practice for future-proofing is to design with a mobile-first approach. Each custom theme for WordPress that the EliTechWiz team creates is fully responsive. This means that the design is optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop with no performance issues. 

Our experts think about how to customize layouts, navigation, images, and interactions for each screen size so that people can easily visit your site and take action regardless of the gadget. Each process involved testing across many devices and browsers to discover any issues before launch. You’ll get a fast, accessible, and consistent website that keeps your users fully engaged.

Can you work with our in-house developers or IT team?

Absolutely. We’re big believers in collaboration. If you already have an internal development or IT team, EliTechWiz will support your process and work together with your tools, schedules, and methods of communication. Our team can lead on design, frontend development, or just specific parts of your WordPress tasks. 

We will provide well-structured code, clear and user-centered design, brand identity, and complete documentation to facilitate a seamless handoff. Need regular check-ins, additional support, dev-ready components in Figma? No problem. We make it easy for your in-house team to take over, scale, or maintain the project with confidence. EliTechWiz makes your team's life easier, not gives them more to worry about!

Do you provide SEO optimization during the design and development process?

Yes. We structure your custom theme with clean, semantic code, fast load times, mobile responsiveness, and accessibility best practices. All of these factors directly impact search rankings.

If you need an optimized heading structure, metadata setup, alt text for images, and performance tuning, our team can assist you with these tasks. We also ensure your theme is fully compatible with popular SEO tools, so your team can easily manage ongoing content optimization. You’ll get a site that’s search-ready from the first launch day and built to help your content perform.
