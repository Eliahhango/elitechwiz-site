FAQ

Can you modernize our legacy enterprise platform with an updated UI/UX?

Sure! If your current system has poor usability, inconsistent design, or outdated tech, we help transform it into a modern, intuitive, and scalable experience without disrupting your business operations.

We audit your existing platform to identify technical limitations, user pain points, and business objectives. Then, our team redesigns the entire user experience with a focus on clarity, accessibility, and performance. Every update is grounded in data, from stakeholder interviews and behavioral analytics to user testing and industry benchmarking.

What’s included in a typical modernization project:

  • Complete UI/UX redesign based on current best practices
  • Information architecture restructuring for better navigation
  • Responsive and accessible design for all devices
  • Design systems for long-term consistency across teams
  • Seamless handoff to development or support for implementation
  • Optional frontend rebuild using modern frameworks

What industries do you specialize in for enterprise web design?

Our business website design agency specializes in web design for high-growth, high-complexity industries where performance, trust, and scalability matter most. These include:

  • AI. Clear, user-driven design for AI tools, platforms, and dashboards that turn advanced logic into simple UX.
  • Fintech and banking. Secure, compliant websites for digital banking, payments, and financial SaaS platforms.
  • Web3. Trusted design for wallets, exchanges, blockchain, and other bold apps that need transparency and clarity.
  • Healthcare and Healthtech. User experiences with intuitive, accessible UX for patients and providers.
  • Edtech. Scalable and engaging systems for online learning platforms and educational tools.
  • SaaS and B2B Tech. Scalable websites designed to convert for complex, multi-user enterprise platforms.

Each industry has its own technical challenges, user expectations, and compliance needs. And our mission is to tailor every project to match. From enterprise-level architecture to conversion-focused UX, we are here for you to perform and scale.

How do you tailor your design process for enterprise-level businesses?

We don’t apply a one-size-fits-all approach. Instead, we customize each phase of the project to your company’s structure, workflows, approval cycles, and digital maturity.

Here’s how we tailor the process for enterprise-level organizations:

1. Strategic discovery with stakeholders

We begin with in-depth stakeholder workshops, cross-department interviews, and a technical discovery phase to uncover your goals, user needs, and internal constraints.

2. Scalable design architecture

EliTechWiz designers map complex user journeys, site structures, and permission-based content flows with scalability in mind.

3. Built-in compliance and accessibility

Our UX/UI decisions account for GDPR, HIPAA, WCAG, and other relevant compliance standards from day one.

4. Design systems for cross-team consistency

We create reusable components, design tokens, and UI kits that make it easier for your internal teams to stay visually and functionally consistent as your platform evolves.

5. Transparent communication & approval structure

You’ll have a dedicated project manager who coordinates with your stakeholders, manages feedback loops, and keeps the project moving forward without bottlenecks.

6. Integration-ready outputs

Our deliverables are development-ready and easy to integrate with your systems.

EliTechWiz process is fast, collaborative, and structured for scale, so you can enjoy beautiful design, a smooth rollout path, and long-term usability across departments.

Can you help align the website design with our internal brand guidelines?

​​Absolutely, it’s a standard part of our enterprise web design services. No matter if you have a full brand system, partial documentation, or evolving guidelines, we ensure the final product reflects your brand identity with precision and consistency.

During the discovery phase, we review your brand assets: logo usage, typography, color systems, tone of voice, imagery, and UI specifications. Our design team then translates these into a digital interface that stays on-brand.

Here’s how we approach it:

  • Full audit of your existing brand materials
  • Digital adaptation of typography, color, and layout systems
  • UI kits and components built on brand design rules
  • Interactive elements styled to feel cohesive with the rest of your ecosystem
  • Close collaboration with your internal marketing or brand team to ensure accuracy

We also help evolve your brand system where needed. For example, creating missing UI patterns, defining responsive behavior, or extending guidelines into a design system that your team can scale across platforms.

Can you conduct stakeholder interviews or user research as part of the process?

Yes! We conduct stakeholder interviews with key decision-makers across departments (product, marketing, operations, and IT). Such conversations help us identify strategic priorities, internal challenges, and areas where the website needs to support cross-functional goals. It also ensures early alignment and reduces the risk of rework later in the process.

Then, our EliTechWiz team carries out qualitative and quantitative research with user interviews, surveys, and usability testing. We analyze heatmaps, session recordings, and behavioral patterns to identify friction points and opportunities. These insights inform the UX structure, content strategy, and interaction design.

How long does an enterprise web design project typically take?

The timeline usually ranges from 8 to 20 weeks. It depends on the complexity, the number of pages, the integrations, and the level of internal stakeholder involvement. But our enterprise web designers always tailor the process to match your business needs, internal timelines, and approval cycles.

Here’s a general breakdown of what that timeline looks like:

1. Discovery & strategy (1–2 weeks)

We begin with stakeholder interviews, competitor analysis, UX audits (if applicable), and technical discovery. This phase defines user journeys, content structure, integration needs, and overall strategy.

2. UX/UI design (3–6 weeks)

Our design team creates user flows, wireframes, interactive prototypes, and high-fidelity UI designs tailored to your brand and users. For enterprise projects, this may also include accessibility planning, localization design, and stakeholder review rounds.

3. Development & integration (4–6 weeks)

We translate approved designs into clean, scalable code, build frontend systems, integrate CMS platforms (such as Webflow or WordPress), connect CRMs, ERPs, and analytics tools, and handle performance optimization.

For more complex builds, timelines may extend to 16-20 weeks. We provide a detailed project plan at kickoff, with weekly check-ins and milestone-based reviews to keep everything transparent and on track.

Can you integrate with existing enterprise systems like CRM or ERP?

Yes! We make sure your website works smoothly with the systems your team already uses. No matter if that's a CRM like Salesforce or HubSpot, an ERP like SAP or Microsoft Dynamics, or even a custom internal platform.

Our team analyzes your data flows, what each team needs, and where everything should connect. We handle the technical side (securely syncing forms, leads, user activity, or content), so your tools all talk to each other without extra manual work.

And don’t worry about security or compliance. We follow strict enterprise standards for encryption, authentication, and access control to keep your data protected.

Can you create custom user dashboards or portals for enterprise users?

Absolutely! We design and develop fully customized user dashboards and portals that meet your users’ needs and your business goals. You’ll get secure, intuitive, and scalable interfaces that deliver real-time data, streamline operations, and enhance the overall digital experience.

These dashboards are never off-the-shelf. We collaborate with your stakeholders to identify your user types, business workflows, data requirements, and operational objectives. Based on that discovery, we create a tailored UX/UI architecture that fits your organization’s exact use cases.

Here’s what’s typically included:

  • Role-based access control, so users only see what’s relevant to them
  • Modular dashboards customizable by team or department
  • Live data integration with CRMs, ERPs, or third-party APIs
  • Secure login systems with SSO or multi-factor authentication
  • Notifications, messaging, and task management tools
  • Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile usability

Need a Webflow integration or a custom frontend? We can also adapt to your tech stack and internal processes.

Can you integrate third-party tools like Salesforce, SAP, or Marketo?

Yes. We regularly integrate enterprise websites with tools such as Salesforce, SAP, Marketo, and other critical third-party platforms to ensure your tech stack functions as a seamless system. If you need to sync user data, automate lead flows, or connect your CRM, ERP, or marketing tools, the EliTechWiz team builds secure, reliable integrations tailored to your workflows.

Our process includes:

  • Reviewing your systems and defining integration goals
  • Using APIs or custom connectors for seamless data sync
  • Securing connections with proper authentication and encryption
  • Automating key actions like form submissions, user segmentation, or campaign tracking
  • Testing and monitoring post-launch to ensure long-term stability

We make sure tools work intelligently together to reduce manual effort and drive measurable business impact.
