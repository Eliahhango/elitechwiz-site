FAQ

How fast can EliTechWiz create a WOW website for my business?

The timeline depends on the scope of work and project complexity. On average, our EliTechWiz team delivers a WOW website in 6 to 14 weeks. How exactly:

  • Discovery and research (1-2 weeks). Our team conducts competitor and market analyses, provides insights into target audiences, and aligns goals.
  • Branding (2,5-6 weeks). If you need a unique, modern, strong, and trustworthy brand identity, we create a full brand platform with logo, color system, typography, and messaging.
  • UX/UI Design (3–5 weeks). We design wireframes, prototypes, and conduct user testing to validate flows. Then, you’ll get responsive layouts and a UI style guide that meets your business goals and helps you scale your website.
  • Development & integrations (3-5 weeks). If you don’t have your own dev team and don’t want to spend resources searching for the best development vendor, EliTechWiz also offers WOW websites development services. It helps you cut time to market, save time and resources, and deliver a high-quality product without design-dev gaps.
  • Deployment and support (1–2 weeks). Our experts will do final checks, launch, and post-release optimization.

! If you have an MVP-style website with fewer features, EliTechWiz can launch it in 4-8 weeks.
But if you require complex corporate or SaaS platforms with advanced integrations, we need 16+ weeks.
‍
Would you like to know the exact timeline today? Contact us, and we’ll define your scope, functionality, and integrations.

Will my site genuinely captivate users and showcase my brand personality?

Yes. No matter if you already have an established brand identity or you’re starting from scratch, we build your website to capture attention and express your personality.

  • If you already have branding! Our design team translates your existing brand assets (logo, colors, typography, and messaging) into your website design for a consistent digital experience. This step provides cohesion with your brand and maximizes recognition across all channels.
  • If you don’t have branding yet! The process starts with brand discovery when our brand team analyzes your positioning, voice, and visual identity. It helps us create moodboards, concept directions, and prototypes, and show your values in visuals and interactions.

In both cases, you will get consistent design systems and story-driven layouts. The outcome is a WOW website that connects emotionally with users, strengthens trust, increases brand recall by up to 47%, and boosts conversions.

Can you connect my website to current business platforms and applications?

Yes. We can integrate your website with:

  • CRM systems
  • ERP solutions
  • Payment gateways
  • Analytics tools
  • Marketing automation platforms

With our wow factor web design and development, integrations scale easily as your business grows. This step strengthens operations, saves time, and improves ROI because your digital tools work as one system.

What's my level of participation throughout the design and build phase?

Your participation is essential, but the workload stays on us. We value your time; that’s why you only set business goals, provide brand assets (if available), and give feedback at key milestones. How it looks in reality:

  • At the discovery stage, you share insights on the audience, business goals, competitors, and your taste.
  • At the design and branding stage, you review wireframes, moodboards, and UI concepts.
  • During testing, you validate usability and confirm the final experience.

Most of our clients spend only 2-4 hours per week on reviews and regular calls (but we can discuss it and organize the most comfortable process for you). Because we really care about your success and want the final website to reflect your vision and reach your objectives.

What post-deployment support services do you provide for WOW websites?

After launch, our support focuses on impact in four areas:

  • Performance monitoring (real-time tracking of uptime, speed, and traffic behavior). We identify bottlenecks and apply fixes before they affect users. Clients see 99.9% uptime and up to 20% faster load times after optimization.
  • Security and compliance (regular patches, SSL updates, and compliance checks). This reduces security risks by 70% compared to unmanaged sites.
  • Scalability and integrations (adding new features, APIs, and third-party platforms without downtime). Businesses expand digital capacity 2-3x faster with scalable architecture.
  • SEO and content optimization (ongoing metadata, content, and structural improvements). Clients maintain rankings and achieve up to +43% more organic traffic year over year.

We care not only about a successful launch, but about your website that grows with your business, adapts to market shifts, and drives measurable ROI every quarter after.
