FAQ

What is your typical project timeline?

From the first call to launch, we take about 6 to 12 weeks to complete the full-cycle process. This time varies based on size, features, and complexity. Our ecommerce website designers and developers work together as a team to create a store that is fast, polished, and reliable. Importantly, we will not rush or sacrifice quality.

→ Discovery and planning (1–2 weeks). There is an initial discovery and planning process where we learn about your business, customers, and goals, as well as conduct advanced research and analysis on competitors and strategize accordingly to align with your brand and audience.

→ UI/UX design (2–3 weeks). You will receive wireframes, moodboards, and then the finished designs that include high-fidelity visuals for desktop and mobile.

→ Development (3–5 weeks). Our ecommerce web development service includes frontend and backend development, integrating third-party tools, and ensuring mobile responsiveness.

→ QA and launch (1–2 weeks). We will test functionality, performance of the website, speed, and reliability on various devices, and security concerns for the site and its customers. If all is acceptable, then we will launch your website.

→ Post-launch support (optional). Our EliTechWiz team can remain onboard to help with updates, performance tracking, and any new features, so you never have to ask what is next.

Some projects, like stores focused around landing pages, can launch quickly. Others include more advanced custom features or integrations may take a bit longer. If you require precise time frames for your project, just reach out to us!

Will I be involved in the design and development process?

Yes, absolutely. We believe that the best web design solutions come from transparent collaboration, and we’ll keep you informed throughout the entire project. You’ll be able to see every aspect of the project as we will share everything in Figma, where you can leave comments directly on screens. Our PM will manage everything according to the timelines and updates outlined. We will have bi-weekly sync meetings on Zoom or Google Meet, and our EliTechWiz designers are always available for on-demand calls when needed.

We will guide you through each phase of our process and share what we’re working on. You do not have to get enmeshed in the technical aspect, but your feedback will help us create a store that reflects your vision, brand, and goals. 

Can you integrate advanced features like product personalization or subscriptions?

Yes, we can, and we do it often in our projects. Our team builds advanced features tailored to your business model. It will be fully integrated solutions that feel seamless to your customers.

  • Product personalization (custom text, colors, images, preview tools)
  • Subscription models (monthly plans, recurring billing, account management)
  • Tiered pricing or bundles
  • User dashboards, loyalty points, referral systems

We’ll define the best approach because our goal is to make sure everything works smoothly, looks great, and delivers a better experience for your users.

Can you design custom product pages or category filters?

Absolutely. Our ecommerce web design firm creates only custom product pages and filtering systems because they must match just your brand. You’ll get product pages that highlight what makes your offer unique: visuals, features, reviews, or pricing logic. We also build smart filtering systems that help customers find what they need faster (by size, color, type, budget, or any custom attribute).

What you can get:

  • Custom layouts for product pages (with upsells, videos, specs, etc.)
  • Dynamic filtering by category, tag, price, or custom attribute
  • Mobile-friendly filters that work smoothly on small screens
  • SEO-friendly structure and performance-optimized code

Better UX means better results! Our clients often see a 15-30% boost in conversion rates just by improving product discovery and page clarity. We make sure your store converts too.

Can you migrate my store from another platform?

Yes, we can migrate your store without losing your data or search rankings. We're an experienced ecommerce web development firm that handles migrations from different platforms – Wix, WooCommerce, Shopify, Magento, BigCommerce, or custom-built systems.

Our processes:

  • Transferring all products, categories, customer data, and orders
  • Redirecting old URLs to protect your SEO
  • Rebuilding the frontend with a fresh design or matching your current look
  • Integrating your tools (payment gateways, CRMs, ERPs, etc.)
  • Thorough testing before go-live

We also take care of performance tuning and mobile responsiveness during the move. Your store migration will be smooth and stress-free.

Can you help with ecommerce conversion rate optimization?

Of course, we focus on it from the very beginning. Our team designs and develops online stores to drive real results: more clicks, more adds to cart, more checkouts.

What we do to increase CRO:

  • User-focused design that guides shoppers through a logical journey
  • Frictionless checkout with fewer steps and smart form fields
  • Mobile-first approach to boost performance across devices
  • Clear calls to action and visual hierarchy that encourage action
  • A/B testing to compare variations and see what works best
  • Analytics and heatmaps to find drop-off points and optimize them

We also use real data to adjust layouts, CTAs, and page structure. In many cases, our clients see a 15–40% increase in conversions after applying our UX and CRO improvements. Our ecommerce web design company will help you turn traffic into paying customers, just contact us.

Do you offer mobile-first design for ecommerce websites?

Yes. We understand your ecommerce traffic comes mostly from smartphones, so your store needs to work ideally on small screens. That’s why our team designs with mobile in mind from the start. You’ll get clean layouts, fast load times, easy tap targets, and checkout flows that don’t make your customers pinch, zoom, or frustrated.

What you will get:

  • Touch-friendly navigation and product browsing
  • Optimized images and page speed for better performance
  • Mobile-specific UI patterns that increase conversions
  • Fully responsive layouts across all screen sizes

What ecommerce platforms do you work with?

EliTechWiz works with a wide range of ecommerce platforms, so we can recommend what fits your business best based on your goals.

Here are some of the platforms:

  • Shopify and Shopify Plus. Great for fast launch, easy management, and scaling without heavy dev work.
  • WooCommerce. Perfect for WordPress users who want full flexibility and control.
  • BigCommerce. A good option for larger stores that need built-in features and API access.
  • Magento/Adobe Commerce. Powerful for complex, enterprise-level ecommerce with custom logic.
  • Custom builds. When your business model doesn’t fit the previously mentioned solutions, we build tailored platforms from the ground up.

You don’t need to handle this alone because our team will help you choose the right stack based on your product type, team, budget, and goals.

Do you provide support for multi-currency stores?

We do support multi-currency setups for ecommerce companies looking to sell across borders. Your customers will be able to shop in their local currency with clear pricing and an easy checkout experience.

What we provide:

  • Automatic or manual currency switcher
  • Real-time updates for exchange rates
  • Flawless experience from product review to payment
  • Platform-specific solutions
  • SEO friendly setup to avoid duplicate content

If you're looking to expand, or if you are currently selling cross-border, we will help you set up your store to succeed locally and globally.

Can you integrate third-party tools like Klaviyo, ShipStation, or Stripe?

Yes.  EliTechWiz is an ecommerce website development company that knows how important it is to connect your store with the tools that make your marketing, logistics, and payments work better and connected.
So, we’ll make sure everything works perfectly together.

This is the short list of what we can integrate:

  • Marketing and email platforms
  • Shipping and fulfillment tools
  • Payment gateways
  • Analytics and CRO tools
  • CRM, ERP, and inventory systems

Our team will help you choose the right tools, connect them to your store, and test. So your data flows correctly, customers stay informed, and your operations run without delays and mistakes. And if you need a custom workflow or API connection, we handle that too.
