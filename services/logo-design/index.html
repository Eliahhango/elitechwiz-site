FAQ

How long does the logo design process usually take?

In most cases, our logo design process takes from 1 to 2 weeks. But the exact timing can vary based on your needs and the project scope. If you also need logo animation, marketing materials, or a full brand identity, the timeline extends (usually by a few extra days). If you need exact timing for your project, just contact our team.

Who owns the copyright to my logo design once completed?

You do, of course! When you approve the final logo and our team completes the project, we hand over full copyright ownership to you. You can use it on your website, app, merch, packaging, ads, well, anywhere. We also provide all the source files and documentation, so you’re fully in control of your brand assets. It’s your logo and your rights.

Can I request changes or additional logo variations after delivery?

Absolutely! If you need changes or extra versions after delivery, our custom logo design company will provide you.

But we usually make sure everything’s on point before we reach the final stage. During the logo design process, you’ll get a full moodboard and logo concept phase with look and feel, metaphors, sketches, and 4 – 6 custom draft options. This step helps us align early, explore directions, and get your feedback upfront, so by the time we deliver the final logo, it already feels right.

Still, if you decide to tweak something or need a variation for a new use case, we’re happy to support.

What types of files will I receive at the end of the logo design project?

You’ll get a complete, ready-to-use package of files, so your logo works everywhere. Also, our brand designers provide you with all assets in one Figma file at the final stage.

If you request our standard logo design service, you’ll get:

  • Vector files (SVG, EPS, PDF). It’s ideal for scaling, printing, or editing.
  • Raster files (PNG, JPG). It’s ready for web, social, and slides.
  • Logo variations in color, black-and-white, icon-only, transparent background.
  • Multiple sizes for headers, favicons, profile images, and other materials.
  • Mini style guide that shows how to use your logo consistently.

If you choose logo animation service, you’ll get:

  • Video formats (MP4, MOV). It’s a great option for intros, pitch decks, YouTube, and socials.
  • Looped GIF that is perfect for lightweight web use.
  • Transparent background version for clean overlays.
  • Editable file on request.

If you need anything else, just ask, and we’ll handle this to make sure your logo works wherever you need it.

How can I get a unique custom logo designed for my company?

It’s simple, you just need to contact a professional logo design company (for example, us - EliTechWiz). We follow a proven, collaborative process to make sure your logo is unique, market-, and goal-oriented.

Our process:

  • Kickoff and brief, where you share your vision, business goals, and target audience (or we can research it for you).
  • Research and moodboard, where our brand designers explore your industry, competitors, and brand style to set the creative direction.
  • Concepts and sketches, where you get 4 – 6 original logo options based on your strategy.
  • Feedback and refinement.
  • Final delivery, where you receive a full package of files to use a logo everywhere.

What should I prepare before starting a logo design project?

To start your logo creation, our team needs some information about your business:

  • A short description of your company (what you do, who you serve, and what makes you different).
  • Your brand name and any tagline if you use one.
  • Your goals for the logo (do you want to feel more premium, more playful, more modern?).
  • Your audience (who are you trying to reach?)
  • Any design preferences (colors you like or dislike, logos you admire, or styles to avoid).
  • Existing assets, if it’s rebranding (old logos, pitch decks, moodboards, etc.).

Don’t worry if you miss some of these. We’ll guide you with questions during our discovery phase and provide you with a brief (all detailed questions will be there). Our goal is to make this process simple, clear, and enjoyable.

What is a logo and why do I need a high-quality one?

A logo is a visual symbol that represents your brand. It’s often made up of text, shapes, colors, or icons, and it’s what people associate with your company (like a brand’s signature). Low-quality or generic logos can confuse people, hurt your reputation, and limit your brand’s potential. A high-quality logo is one of the smartest investments you can make.

You need a high-quality logo because it:

  • Creates a strong first impression.
  • Builds credibility and trust with your audience.
  • Makes your brand easy to remember and recommend.
  • Helps you differentiate among competitors.
  • Supports every process (scaling, marketing, sales, fundraising, hiring, and so on).

What makes an effective and good logo design?

A good and effective logo must tell your brand’s story, connect with your audience, and stick in their memory.

Our approaches to logo creation:

  • Simplicity.
  • Memorability.
  • Relevance.
  • Scalability.
  • Versatility.
  • Timelessness.

It’s the result of strategic thinking, design expertise, and a deep understanding of your brand, market, and people. Our goal is to set your brand apart from just “okay.”
