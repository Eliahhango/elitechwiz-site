Frequently asked questions

What is an Web3 design?

Web3 design creates intuitive and trustworthy experiences for decentralized products. It can be crypto wallets, DeFi dashboards, NFT marketplaces, DAOs, and other platforms. Unlike Web2, its goal is to help users understand complex blockchain interactions (staking, trading, or connecting a wallet).

How long does it take to start a Web3 project?

We can usually start immediately after defining your project goals and requirements.

How much does it cost to create product design for Web3?

The cost of Web3 product design depends on:

  • Bounce rates.
  • Conversion rates.
  • Retention.
  • User satisfaction.

On average, full Web3 UI/UX design projects start around $6K.

What are the challenges in creating design for Web3?

Many users are new to blockchain, so the biggest goal is simplifying complexity without losing transparency or control. Security cues, wallet connections, transaction confirmations, and gas fees need to be clear and intuitive. Visual identity also plays a huge role. Our team has to balance tech-forward innovation with trust and usability.
