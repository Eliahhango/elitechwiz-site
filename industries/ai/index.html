Frequently asked questions

What is an AI design?

AI design focuses on creating interfaces and user experiences that make artificial intelligence usage intuitive, human, and transparent. We pay attention to how people interact with intelligent systems because users should trust the AI’s decisions and understand its outputs.

How long does it take to start an AI project?

We can usually start immediately after defining your project goals and requirements.

How much does it cost to create an artificial intelligence design?

The cost of AI product design depends on the project’s scale, complexity, and data interactions. On average, an AI product design project starts from $6K.

What are the challenges in creating a design for AI?

We define the main challenges for AI design:

  • Making advanced algorithms feel transparent and human.
  • Showing how AI works behind the scenes in a user-friendly way.
  • Ensuring users feel confident interacting with automated systems.
  • Handling live, ever-changing data and still keeping interfaces clear.
  • Designing flows that feel natural and supportive.
