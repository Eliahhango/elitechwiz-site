Editorial policy

Updated on March 10, 2026

At EliTechWiz Agency OÜ, we take pride in our extensive experience in the dynamic world of SEO. With a team of seasoned professionals, our expertise spans a wide range of SEO techniques, advanced strategies, and content methodologies. Having collaborated with companies of all sizes—from emerging startups to industry leaders—we have honed our skills to deliver SEO solutions tailored to the unique needs of each business.

There are always questions that need answers, and we help our clients find them. Let us explain how we create the articles and reviews you rely on when searching for information and making decisions.

Important!

At EliTechWiz Agency OÜ, we are committed to delivering top-quality content created by humans for humans. Publishing AI-generated content using tools such as ChatGPT contradicts our principles.

Principles

At EliTechWiz Agency OÜ, we are committed to delivering top-quality content created by humans for humans. Publishing AI-generated content using tools such as ChatGPT contradicts our principles.

Credibility

Our team always verifies and validates the accuracy of the information we publish. We never share unverified data.

Impartiality

From SEO tool recommendations to selecting an SEO company, we strive to present information objectively and without bias. Our content is thoroughly researched and is not written to support any specific economic or political viewpoint.

Accuracy

Precision and clarity are crucial in the world of SEO. We collaborate with qualified experts to ensure our information is up-to-date and easy to understand. If our clients identify any errors or inaccuracies, we promptly investigate and correct them.

Uniqueness

Every article we produce is unique and original. We never copy content from other sources.

Ethics

We adhere to ethical codes and journalistic standards. EliTechWiz Agency OÜ shares the principles and values outlined in The Estonian Journalists Code of Ethics. Our content follows and upholds the foundations of ethical journalism: accuracy and fairness, minimizing harm, acting independently, and maintaining transparency.

EliTechWiz Agency OÜ's content is intended for informational purposes and is not a substitute for consultations with licensed or certified SEO professionals, lawyers, accountants, financial advisors, or other certified specialists.

Editorial process

Our team of editors and SEO experts writes and manages all articles published on https: // EliTechWiz.agency/ . We do not provide direct SEO consulting to clients but instead offer analysis, insights, and educational information to help them make informed decisions about their initiatives. Where possible, we include practical examples to clarify complex topics and concepts.

Our editorial team continuously reviews site content to identify articles that contain outdated information. These articles are revised and updated or, when necessary, completely rewritten, reviewed, and edited. Our team of experienced SEO professionals ensures our content remains accurate and relevant. Articles that have been updated include a corresponding date to reflect this.

Quality standarts

Every article we create at EliTechWiz Agency OÜ is the result of collective efforts from professionals working together to ensure it is responsible, accurate, clear, useful, and aligned with our editorial principles.

Our editorial team approves all article topics, and our writers have deep expertise in the subject matter they cover. Our team of professional editors and fact-checkers reviews content to ensure it meets our values.

All team members are responsible for disclosing any potential conflicts of interest.

Corrections

EliTechWiz Agency OÜ is committed to informing readers when an error occurs and correcting it. Any corrections will be clearly displayed on the page.

If you believe you have found an error, please contact us at info@elitechwiz.site, and we will address it promptly.

Authors

EliTechWiz Agency OÜ carefully selects, verifies, and recruits a team of experts with high qualifications in various aspects of digital marketing. Experts are chosen based on their knowledge, experience, and ability to communicate complex topics in a clear and accessible manner.

Every article is either written or reviewed by an expert in the relevant field.

The author or reviewer of each article is listed at the top of the page, along with a link to their biography, which provides details about their position, education, professional experience, and social media presence.

EliTechWiz Agency OÜ values transparency and believes readers should know who is behind the content they consume.

All authors are required to adhere to the editorial principles outlined above.

Third-Party content

Third-party content is carefully reviewed by the EliTechWiz Agency OÜ editorial team to ensure it aligns with our policies and standards. Any such content is clearly and prominently labeled to indicate its source.
